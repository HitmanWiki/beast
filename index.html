About

Introducing Based Beast

A returning menace from the multicolor mind of Matt Furie

Based Beast first clawed its way into reality in the 2019 Tuff Crowd collection, snarling with layered satire and surreal menace. Now, this feral icon returns from the shadows in Matt Furie’s trippy new phase — the Cortex Vortex collection.





With glaring eyes, acid-drip gradients, and a distorted beastly snout, Beast Seller is both predator and parable — an animalistic embodiment of consumerism, identity, and ferocity in the digital age. Rendered in Furie's signature ink-heavy psychedelia, this character howls across dimensions.





Matt Furie’s signature weirdness pulses through every vein of this creature, a mutant oracle, a grotesque guru, and the mind-melting heart of Cortex Vortex.

How to buy

Create a wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $Beast. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Visit App.uniswap.org（MetaMask Mobile Features An Integrated Browser) To Connect Your Wallet. Then Paste The $BEAST Address Into The field To Indicate Which Coin You Wish To Exchange Your Eth for.

Swap ETH For $BEAST

Choose the Amount You Wish to Trade, Click "swap," And Confirm The Transaction. When Your Wallet Prompts You, Confirm the Action. Congratulations, You Are Now A Proud Holder Of $BEAST.
